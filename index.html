ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Providing Quality Education Since 1960

Jagat Singh Tyagi Uchtar Madhyamik Vidyalaya, located in Sarai Rehman, Aligarh, is a Government Aided School committed to providing quality education and fostering discipline, knowledge, and character among students.

The school focuses on creating a positive learning environment that encourages academic excellence, cultural values, sportsmanship, and overall personality development.
