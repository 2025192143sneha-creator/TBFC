ACADEMIC OVERVIEW

Excellence in Education

Jagat Singh Tyagi Uchtar Madhyamik Vidyalaya provides quality education with a focus on academic excellence, discipline and character development.

The school follows the Uttar Pradesh State Board curriculum and encourages students to participate in classroom learning, practical activities, cultural programmes and sports.

Our aim is to develop confident learners who are prepared for higher education and responsible citizenship.

CLASSES OFFERED

TEACHING METHODOLOGY

Our Teaching Approach

The school follows a student-centered teaching approach that combines classroom instruction, practical learning, group discussions and activity-based education.

Teachers encourage students to ask questions, think critically and participate actively in classroom activities for better understanding of concepts.

Along with academics, equal importance is given to discipline, moral values, communication skills and personality development.

EXAMINATION SYSTEM

Student Assessment

Unit Tests

Regular unit tests help monitor students' academic progress throughout the session.

Half-Yearly Exams

Comprehensive assessments are conducted during the middle of the academic year.

Annual Examination

Final examinations evaluate the overall learning outcomes of students.

CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Cultural Programmes

Educational Activities

Science exhibitions, quizzes, essay writing and awareness programmes enhance learning.
