OUR HISTORY

About the School

Jagat Singh Tyagi Uchtar Madhyamik Vidyalaya is a Government Aided School located in Sarai Rehman, Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh. The school has served the local community by providing quality education and encouraging discipline, responsibility and moral values among students.

The school aims to provide an environment where students develop academically, socially and personally. Along with classroom learning, students are encouraged to participate in cultural activities, sports and other co-curricular programmes.

Teachers, parents and the school administration work together to support every student's educational journey and overall development.

OUR VISION & MISSION

Guiding Principles

Vision

To provide quality education that helps students become knowledgeable, disciplined, responsible and confident citizens.

Mission

To create a safe and positive learning environment where every student can develop academically, morally and socially.

Core Values

Discipline, honesty, respect, teamwork, equality and continuous learning form the foundation of our educational approach.
